Cloud Computing:

Cloud Computing:
Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services such as storage, servers, databases, networking, and software over the internet.

IaaS:
Infrastructure as a Service provides virtualized computing resources over the internet.

PaaS:
Platform as a Service provides a platform for developing and deploying applications.

SaaS:
Software as a Service provides software applications over the internet.

Cyber Security:

Cyber Security:
Cyber security is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks.

Encryption:
Encryption is the process of converting data into a secure format to prevent unauthorized access.

Firewall:
A firewall is a network security system that monitors and controls incoming and outgoing network traffic.

Internet of Things:

IoT:
Internet of Things refers to the network of physical devices connected to the internet that collect and share data.

Sensors:
Sensors are devices that detect changes in the environment and send data to other devices.

Project Work:

Project:
A project is a practical implementation of knowledge to solve a real-world problem.
